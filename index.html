Magic: The Gathering

One of my favorite games

What is Magic?

  • Magic is a TCG or Trading Card Game where players collect cards to build their own decks they use to play against each other
  • While certain cards allow you to win in specific ways, the most common goal of the game is to reduce your opponents life total to zero by summoning cratures and casting spells
  • While premise itself is simple, my favorite part of the game is the diverse strategies and synergies that can be used when deckbuilding
  • You can play a complicated deck that relies on a multi card combo over several turn combos or a deck as with a plan as simple as "deal a lot of damage to get rid of my opponent and their creatures"
